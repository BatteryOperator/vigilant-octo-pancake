About Blub Blub Studios

Our Mission

Blub Blub Studios LLC creates educational tools with minimal distraction. We believe that learning apps should prioritize education over entertainment, focusing children's attention on skill development rather than flashy features.

Every design decision we make is guided by one question: "Does this help the child learn?" If the answer is no, we remove it.

Our Approach

We build tools, not games. While we want children to enjoy using our apps, our primary goal is effective learning. This philosophy manifests in several ways:

Minimal Distraction Design

We use muted colors, clean layouts, and ample whitespace. Our interfaces are calm and focused, helping children concentrate on the learning content rather than decorative elements.

Purposeful Features

Every feature serves an educational purpose. We don't include unnecessary animations, sounds, or visual effects that could distract from learning.

Privacy First

We respect children's privacy. Our apps don't collect user data, don't include analytics, and don't integrate with third-party services. Learning happens entirely on the device, under parental control.

Research-Based Content

Our educational content is based on proven teaching methods and research in early childhood education. We prioritize effectiveness over novelty.

Our Values

  • Education First - Learning outcomes drive all product decisions
  • Respect for Privacy - Children deserve privacy-first technology
  • Minimal Distraction - Focus enables better learning
  • Quality Over Quantity - Well-designed tools are better than many features
  • Accessibility - Educational tools should work for all learners
