Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Blub Blub Studios LLC, we are committed to protecting the privacy of children and families. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use for all of our applications, including Story Slider.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data from users.

Story Slider and all Blub Blub Studios apps are designed with a privacy-first approach. Specifically:

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not track user behavior or app usage
  • We do not use analytics services or tracking technologies
  • We do not collect device identifiers or location data

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with any third-party services that would collect user data. We do not use:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services for user data
  • Any other third-party data collection tools

Data Storage

All app data, including user progress and preferences, is stored locally on the user's device. This data never leaves the device and is not accessible to Blub Blub Studios or any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are designed for children and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Because we do not collect any personal information, we do not require parental consent for data collection.

Changes to Privacy Practices

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this policy and notify users through the App Store update notes. Any changes that would involve data collection would require user consent.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no user data for us to access, modify, or delete. All data remains under your control on your device.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@blubblubstudios.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
