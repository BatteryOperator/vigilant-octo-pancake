Our Mission

We believe that educational technology should prioritize learning over entertainment. Every design decision we make asks: "Does this help the child learn?" If not, we remove it.

Our apps use muted colors, clear layouts, and purposeful interactions to keep children focused on what matters most: developing their skills and confidence.

Privacy First

We respect the privacy of children and families. Story Slider and all future Blub Blub Studios apps are designed with a privacy-first approach:

  • No user data collection
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertisements
  • No third-party integrations
